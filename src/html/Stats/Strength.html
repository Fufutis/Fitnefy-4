Strength Workouts

  1. Bench Press

    Description: Targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps.

    Reps/Sets: 4 sets of 8-12 reps

  2. Deadlifts

    Description: Works the entire posterior chain including back, glutes, and hamstrings.

    Reps/Sets: 4 sets of 6-8 reps

  3. Squats

    Description: Primarily targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes.

    Reps/Sets: 4 sets of 8-12 reps

  4. Overhead Shoulder Press

    Description: Focuses on the shoulders and triceps.

    Reps/Sets: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

  5. Bicep Curls

    Description: Isolates the biceps for targeted strength building.

    Reps/Sets: 4 sets of 10-15 reps

  6. Tricep Dips

    Description: Targets the triceps and chest.

    Reps/Sets: 4 sets of 8-12 reps

  7. Pull-Ups

    Description: Works the back, shoulders, and biceps.

    Reps/Sets: 4 sets to failure

  8. Leg Press

    Description: Focuses on the quadriceps, glutes, and hamstrings.

    Reps/Sets: 4 sets of 10-12 reps

  9. Barbell Rows

    Description: Targets the back muscles, particularly the lats.

    Reps/Sets: 4 sets of 8-10 reps

  10. Lunges

    Description: Engages the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes.

    Reps/Sets: 4 sets of 10-12 reps per leg
